The purpose of the app

The purpose of the app is basically to give access to all the people who are on the net, Get the information about fires and draw conclusions from this information and give them an option to give reports that will go to firefighters who can respond quickly. In this way firefighters also have the information flowing from the people and people also have the information available from firefighters

About Us

We worked on the project Three partners We wanted all people to have access to the information available regarding fires in the country. It was important to us and we believe that firefighters also have a great need to receive the information regarding reports and the biggest source of this information is the people with the convenience of the app. We believe this app will make a national change for the better regarding firefighting
